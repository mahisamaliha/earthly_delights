  • Trowel. Like a spade or hoe, a gardening trowel is used for digging small holes and moving soil.

FERTILIZERS

  • Organic fertilizers (OF) Organic fertilizers are natural fertilizers obtained from plants and animals. They enrich the soil with carbon compounds needed for plant activities. Organic fertilizers increase the soil’s organic matter content, promote the reproduction and activities of microorganisms and alter the physical and chemical properties of the soil.
  • Inorganic fertilizers. Inorganic fertilizers are chemical fertilizers that contain nutrients necessary for the growth of crops produced by chemical methods.
